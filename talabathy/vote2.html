Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) — Indian Political Party TVK primarily refers to Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (தமிழக வெற்றி கழகம்), a regional political party in Tamil Nadu (and the union territory of Puducherry) in India. It was **founded on 2 February 2024 by popular Tamil film actor-turned-politician Vijay, who is the party’s president. Wikipedia Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam literally means Tamil Nadu Victory Federation. Abbreviation: TVK. Founder & President: Vijay (actor) General Secretary: N. Anand Treasurer: P. Venkataraman Headquarters in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The party emphasizes social justice, Tamil identity, egalitarianism, and clean governance. TVK aims to contest Tamil Nadu’s 2026 Legislative Assembly elections as an alternative to established parties like DMK and AIADMK. The News Medium Its slogan “Pirappokkum Ellaa Uyirkkum” (meaning roughly “All are born equal”) reflects its focus on equality. Wikipedia The party has organised rallies, built grassroots structures (e.g., booth committee secretaries), and grown a large supporter base across the state. News Room Odisha TVK is in active alliance negotiations with other political groups ahead of the 2026 elections. The Times of India The party has publicly criticised institutions like the CBFC in relation to cultural issues (e.g., actor Vijay’s film release).
